ABout us

The Goal.

We want to develop tools and resources from the ground up for a passionate community of do'ers. This means creating a baseline by working with 3rd party tools, events and agencies to curate opportunities while we test and build our own with all of ya'll.

Slash Platform

Biggest part of this is getting off something like an email newsletter and onto something that's dynamic. A place that you can upload almost anything and showcase it. Past this it's also a place to gain feedback and inspiration while also being able to give/receive opportunities to the social communities around you all curated into one place.

Slash.TV

We're really excited to release Slash.TV in Q3 of 2019. Slash TV will be a place of all content big or small that we feel like is relevant to our community. We've already started developing in-house content as well as working with other content developers to develop some for the platform. We hope this will be a game changer for open content that's meant to hoan in on your skills or open the door to new ones.
